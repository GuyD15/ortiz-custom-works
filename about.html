About Ortiz Custom Works

Ortiz Custom Works is a custom cabinetry company built on over 30 years of craftsmanship, precision, and technical expertise.

We specialize in custom cabinetry for every room, closets, kitchens, pantries, built-ins, offices, garages, and laundry room as well as staircase upgrades. Our focus is simple: delivering refined, high-quality solutions that enhance and complete thoughtfully designed homes.

With a strong foundation in drafting and technical design, every project is carefully planned and engineered to ensure seamless integration, clean lines, and lasting durability. Many homeowners come with ideas and inspiration, we transform those concepts into thoughtfully designed, highly functional spaces built to perform as beautifully as they look.

At Ortiz Custom Works, we are committed to delivering "Customized Spaces for Your Home" crafted with intention, built with integrity, and installed with precision.
